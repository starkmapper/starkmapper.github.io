# Brilliant Bingo
 ## needs to be re-translated from the dutch version!
 
## A low-threshold invitation to special connections through in-depth conversations

### Requirements

/// tab | Physical

1. Special people (the more, the better)
2. Open space where people can easily walk or move around
3. Bingo cards: start with milder, general ones, and adapt them to your audience.
   (AI works wonders).
4. Pencils/pens/markers
5. Optional: Dice to determine the next question.
///

/// tab | Online

1. Special people (the more, the better)
2. Online meeting space with breakout options (Use what you have!):
   * Zoom
   * Discord
   * Teams
   * Slack
3. Online Whiteboard or PDF
4. Laptop/tablet/phone
5. Optional: Put the questions in an online wheel of fortune/names
///

!!! Note
    Create "spaces" per theme: a "hobby" table, a "schooling" table, a "family" table, etc.
    This gives people the freedom to choose whether or not to discuss a particular theme,
    which contributes to safety.

### Why it works

This game challenges you to find people you think are special.
It activates your curiosity to truly get to know people better.
This genuine curiosity helps people connect.
By looking for something you find special, you help the other person feel appreciated for something they themselves take for granted.
